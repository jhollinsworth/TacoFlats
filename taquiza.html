Taquiza Catering

The Taquiza is the cornerstone of our catering services, and rightfully so. The Tortillas are made in-house from our very own tortilleria and are then cooked on site on a hot griddle, and the Al Pastor is carved from our own mobile "trompo", so that your guests can enjoy the show that a Taquiza party offers.

We know any special event is both fun and tedious at the same time, so let us take care of the hard work so that you could focus on the important thing, your special event!

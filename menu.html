Our approach on food is simple, homemade traditional Mexican appetizers and tacos.

• We make everything from scratch, we don’t freeze, and we certainly don’t microwave. Our chips and tostadas are made in house, and our corn and flour tortillas are handmade. We even offer a gluten-free and paleo friendly option in the jicama tortilla, which we thinly slice, marinade, and serve with your favorite taco.

• We roast the al pastor on a vertical rotisserie “trompo”, our carnitas are deep fried then slow roasted for hours, and our chicken and beef options are grilled over an open flame. Our vegetarian and vegan friendly menu offers appetizers and tacos made with vegan refried black beans, fresh spinach, eggs, and all sorts of grilled and fresh veggies. As for seafood options, we make some really interesting tuna tostadas along with some fish tacos seasoned with a house made blackening, or our traditional al pastor marinade. We source our seafood from the one and only, Quality Seafood.

Our approach on food is simple, homemade traditional Mexican appetizers and tacos.

• We make everything from scratch, we don’t freeze, and we certainly don’t microwave. Our chips and tostadas are made in house, and our corn and flour tortillas are handmade. We even offer a gluten-free and paleo friendly option in the jicama tortilla (a Mexican yam bean), which we thinly slice, marinade, and serve with your favorite taco.

• We roast the al pastor on a vertical rotisserie “trompo”, our carnitas are deep fried then slow roasted for hours, and our chicken and beef options are grilled over an open flame. We source our seafood from the one and only Quality Seafood right here in Austin, Texas.

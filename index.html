Taquiza

The most recognizable Mexican food item is probably the taco. We love them so much that we have parties just to celebrate them. These parties are called “Taquizas”. Taquizas are informal gatherings where people stand around the grill or the table with tortillas in hand ready to make their own tacos. There’s always different kinds of fillings and salsas, but probably the most common ones are carne asada and pico de gallo. We've mastered the art of the Taquiza, bringing the authenticity of a great Mexican tradition to your celebration. Contact us now for catering options!
